About

The best coffee is the one you have not tasted yet.

Welcome to the Dazzling Cafe, newly opened in Smithfield, Dublin, Ireland!

We honestly believe that the coffee world is boundless and barely explored by the everyday regular drinker. There are plenty of spots in the city where you can grab a cup and mozzy on, but... Forget about the commercial coffee haunts - we are talking about inviting you into a sacred shrine, a dedicated research laboratory, a pleasant environment where your curiosity (and tastebuds) are encouraged. Savouring a properly prepared cup of coffee is a true wonder, a universe on the verge of hedonism. You should know that coffee is used beyond beverages as a key ingredient for cakes, desserts sand savoury dishes. As we all know, coffee is a rich in variation. However, one must know how to appreciate and enjoy it as it deserves.

Do you want to drink something unique and unforgettable? This coffee experience workshop offers tastings, courses and talks hosted by talented, internationally recognized baristas and coffee growers. Come meet experts introducing the intricacies that the coffee world offers. From the beans to your cup ... There are so many kinds of coffees that you will be able to enjoy! Ideal for gourmands and adventurious tasters, you will discover mixtures of flavors and tastes unsuspected. Your taste buds will be delighted!

Coffee Tastings & Courses

Please review the courses available and call us to reserve your spot! Payments are accepted on the premises only.
Experience Description Attendance cost (per person)

Coffee - from the bean to your table

This tasting is great for groups who enjoy a thorough approach to what they eat and drink. A great overview of the history, cultivation, and production techniques involved in the making of coffee. Learn the difference between different coffee types and use all five senses to revel in the flavours of each bean, with or without milk. 200 euros

A Technical Approach to Coffee

Improvise yourself as a scientist and work with our baristas to master the various brewing types and techniques applicable to coffee. You will be exposed to new coffee brewing technologies, along with tools of all sorts to facilitate your experimentation. Course up to two hours. 250 euros

An Exploration of Brazil through Coffee

Organic promoters and growers from Brazil come to talk about their experience and lives. Did you know that 30% of all coffee beans in the world come from Brazil? This tasting can be organised for groups of 5 or more during the weekends. 175 euros

Afternoon 'Buzz'

 If you like to chat among friends and nibble on tasty things, this is the perfect tasting for you! You will be invited to sit and enjoy specific pairings best suited with the coffee blends available. Cocktails, savoury bites and delicacies are on the menu. 30 euros per hour

Cafe Latte - Level 1

Beginner level skill and techniques in preparation for the Irish competititon of latte art. Course up to 4 hours, divided between two evenings. 150 euros

Cafe Latte - Level 2

Intermediate to advanced level skill and techniques in preparation for the Irish competititon of latte art. Course up to 4 hours, divided between two evenings. 200 euros

We offer snacks and freshly baked goods on a daily basis. We can cater to vegan, lactose-intolerant and coeliac/gluten-intolerant customers.

In-house news: We now offer Chemex coffee!

The Chemex is what all those other coffeemakers strive to be. Invented in 1941, it has never needed to be altered or improved upon. Winning awards in both the design and science communities, being kept in permanent collection of some of the most prestigious museums in the world, are just a couple pieces of its iconic history that none of the others can boast. Widely considered to be one of the best ways to brew the perfect cup of coffee; the Chemex has been the choice of coffee lovers globally for over 75 years and counting. (Extract from Chemex' official website)
